good morning

Rise and shine! Or, you know, just rise. "Good morning" is often a polite suggestion, not a statement of fact, especially before your first cup. Here are some delightful truths about dawn: * That "beautiful sunrise"? It's just the universe mocking your desperate need for more sleep, a daily reminder that the darkness didn't win... yet. * Your beloved snooze button isn't about rest; it’s a tiny, desperate rebellion against the crushing inevitability of adulting. * That first coffee isn't a beverage. It's a pharmacological intervention, a vital life-support system preventing you from becoming a highly articulate, perpetually grumpy zombie. * Morning people? They’re either aliens or running on the sheer, unadulterated terror of wasting a single moment of their finite existence. Embrace the gloom; it’s all part of the ritual. Now, go forth and grudgingly exist!
